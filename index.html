Popular destinations in Turkey
Hagia Sophia
Hagia Sophia is a former Byzantine church, later Ottoman mosque and now museum in Istanbul. It is one of the most famous buildings in the world and is considered to be a masterpiece of Byzantine architecture.
Mount Ararat
Mount Ararat is a large dormant volcano in eastern Turkey. It is the highest mountain in Turkey and is traditionally considered to be the landing place of Noah's Ark.
Agora of Smyrna
Visit the Agora of Smyrna, an ancient Greek marketplace that has been excavated and restored.
